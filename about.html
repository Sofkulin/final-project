ABOUT US

We are a small queer-owned and -operated gaming cafe in the center of Helsinki, Finland! We offer a variety of beverages, baked goods and other delicous treats. The happy gaymer offers daily videogames, boardgames and also hosts table top roleplaying events once a week on Thursdays. We are open from 07:30-22:00 Mondays through Fridays and 09:00-23:00 Saturday-Sunday. You can find us at [ADDRESS].

The Happy Gaymer was opened in 2022 and was created by founder Sof Lindberg and their team. We are a queer-owned and operated business which provides a safe space for everyone no matter their sexuality, gender, age, ethnicity or religion.

The Happy Gamer also does coffee-catering for events, for more information contact: sof@happygaymer.com
