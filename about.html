ABOUT US

Cutea is a small queer-owned and -operated bubble-tea cafe in the center of Seattle, Washington! We offer a variety of bubble teas, cold and warm beverages, baked goods and many other delicous treats.

We are open from 07:30-22:00 Mondays through Fridays and 09:00-23:00 Saturday-Sunday. You can find us at [ADDRESS].

Cutea was opened in 2022 and was created by founder Sof Lindberg and their team. We are a queer-owned and operated business which provides a safe space for everyone no matter their sexuality, gender, age, ethnicity or religion.

Cutea also does coffee- and tea-catering for events, for more information contact: sof@cutea-boba.com

Sof Lindberg, 2022
